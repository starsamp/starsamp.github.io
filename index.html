Who We Are

Where Aerospace Meets Gastronomy

Headquartered in Brussels, SPACESAMP transforms astronaut food science into premium civilian nutrition — without sacrificing natural flavor.

The Future of Food, Today

SPACESAMP is a Brussels-based space food development company dedicated to civilianizing astronaut nutrition. We integrate advanced food processing, health-optimized ingredients, and dietary supplementation into a holistic approach that's both scientific and delicious.

Our philosophy bridges cutting-edge aerospace technology with a deep respect for natural ingredients. We believe the best nutrition should be standardized for safety and efficiency — yet never compromise on taste or the innate qualities of whole foods.

Our Mission

Nourishing Earth, Reaching for Stars

SPACESAMP is committed to environmental stewardship, carbon reduction, and advancing open-source food innovation. We adopt a community-synced, collaborative approach to building trustworthy, safe, and efficient fast-moving consumer food channels.

While standardizing for scale, we never abandon the natural essence and deliciousness at the heart of real food.
